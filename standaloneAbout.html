Lifegraph Labs Seamlessly persisting physical interactions.

We’re six engineering students from Olin College in Needham, MA working to enable everyday people to build apps to connect their physical and virtual interactions. We are introducing a platform for developers to persist, record, and share physical interactions on Facebook's Open Graph.

Facebook’s Open Graph is an extension of its vast social graph- it allows users’ activity on third-party websites and pages throughout the web to be naturally integrated into their Facebook experience.

Lifegraph Labs was born from the idea that much of the lives we live, and the activities we wish to remember and share with our friends, are not done online. Oftentimes there is a fundamental disconnect between how people express themselves online and how they express themselves in everyday interactions.
